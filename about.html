GASKET COMPANY IN INDIA

Talbros Sealing Materials is an integrated sealing solution and gasket company in India, manufacturing the following products out of it’s various facilities- Gasketing and Sealing Materials like cork rubber material, rubber molded gaskets, edge bonded gasket, edge molded gaskets, non asbestos gaskets, anti vibration pads and rubber mattings. Our beginnings can be traced back to 1981 when we established a cork rubber plant to feed the gasketing industry. Since then we have diversified into manufacturing a wide range of sealing materials as well as a soft gasket cutting operation which is expanding rapidly. Headquartered out of India with a warehousing facility in the US and distributors for our products located globally, our company belongs to the BNT Talbros Group which comprises of 6 companies with 14 locations and over 1700 employees. Approximately 70% of the company’s revenues are derived from various industries in the US market. What started out as a rubber-cork manufacturing operation has expanded to a full fledged soft gasket cutting company with the capability to manufacture finished gaskets in cork, rubber, fiber and non-asbestos. Our gasketing material operations continue to this date and we are still one of the preferred sources of supply for cork rubber material, rubber molded sheet material, nitrile rubber sheeting as well as different types of rubber matting globally. We are always aiming to expand into different materials and look at forming partnerships with companies to explore this
